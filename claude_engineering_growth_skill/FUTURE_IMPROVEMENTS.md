## Why Not Add These Now

Adding too much project-specific detail too early can make the skill stale. The better approach is to keep the core skill stable and add project references after the architecture settles.

## Maintenance Rule

Whenever a difficult bug is solved, add one short case study:

- Symptom.
- Evidence.
- Root cause.
- Fix.
- Lesson.

This turns debugging history into reusable engineering knowledge.
